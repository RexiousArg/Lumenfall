# Aevum's Prism and The Guide — Lore Notes

Aevum's Prism is the rarest card in Lumenfall, but it is not a prize. It is a **test of weight**.

- Each use offers outsized gains and invisible costs.
- Using it down to zero charges invites Blight and its consequences.
- Trying to circumvent its limits (stacking Prisms) triggers Greed.

The Guide is not a god and not a narrator. They are the first being to truly understand Lumenfall from the inside.

- Once an ordinary hero, forced into a glitch.
- Saw their own code, their limits, and the patterns of the world.
- Emerged changed: not omniscient, but awake.

The Sharenbaal carries echoes of the Guide's understanding, but the Guide remains a late-game encounter whose tone and insight depend on the player's own path.

All mechanical details for Prism and Guide behavior will be defined in dedicated future specs; this file is only mythic framing.
