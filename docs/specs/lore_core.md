# Lumenfall — Core Lore Notes

Lumenfall is not just a CCG or an ARPG; it is a world that learns the player.

- The **CCG** side speaks to collection, pattern, and probability.
- The **ARPG** side speaks to motion, timing, and survival.
- The **philosophical endgame** speaks to recognition: seeing the patterns behind your own choices.
