# Regions and Classes — Lore Notes

This document describes tone and narrative identity for regions and classes.

## Kalveth Esthrel — Stormbound

A frontier esthrel where storms coil over jagged horizons. Stormbound learn to read the sky like scripture.

## Velun's Reach — Lightrisen

Highlands bathed in pale-gold light. The Lightrisen live between dawn and noon, sworn to bring clarity where the world is dim.

## Wraithmarsh — Shadowrune

Marshes where sound folds in on itself. Shadowrunes write sigils into fog, bending absence into a weapon.

Further regions are intentionally left unnamed here until their mechanics and narrative are locked.
