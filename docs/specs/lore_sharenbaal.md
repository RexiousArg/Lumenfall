# Sharenbaal — Understood Depth

The Sharenbaal is not a codex **about** the world; it is a codex that adjusts to the one reading it.

- Appears at level 5 with no fanfare.
- Grows as the player acts, not as they read.
- Never tells you what you are; it shows you what you have already been doing.

Each chapter is a lens:
- Elements: how you interact with raw forces.
- Regions: where you choose to linger.
- Classes: which roles you gravitate toward.
- Ways Walked: which of the eight behavioral paths your actions align with.

The text here is only flavor; see `sharenbaal.yaml` for mechanical rules.
