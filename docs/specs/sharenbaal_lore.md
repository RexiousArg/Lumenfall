# Lumenfall – The Sharenbaal (Lore Canon v1)

*“Some worlds are written after they are made.  
Lumenfall was written while it was still remembering how to exist.”*

The Sharenbaal is not a book in the normal sense. It is a **geometry of memory** that chose the shape of a book so lesser minds would not break trying to look at it.

When players first see it, it is simple:

A dark, galactic volume, edges frayed with starlight, hovering just above their hands.

When they open it, the illusion of paper persists just long enough for comfort.
Then the page becomes a **horizon**.
